Service Opporitunities

The Seattle Academy Marine Science Club is all about community service and helping to protect our local marine environment. Throughout the year we will have many special service opporitunities in order to help our local seattle area.

Weekly Meetings

We meet in STR No.501 on Wednesdays, here we talk about upcoming service opporitunities and sometimes just have fun group activities.

About Us

The Seattle Academy Marine Biology Club is a club based aroudn the marine environment of Seattle and the general well-being of the marine life that lives next to us. We conduct service trips and fundraisers in order to assist in the protection of our local sealife.
